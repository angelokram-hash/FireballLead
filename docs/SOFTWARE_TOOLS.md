## Empfohlener Minimal-Stack (Budget: ~150€/Monat)

1. **HubSpot CRM Free** — Lead-Erfassung, E-Mail, Pipeline (0€)
2. **Vercel** — Landing Pages hosten (0€)
3. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** — Lead-Suche (79€/Mo)
4. **Google Ads** — Intent-basierte Leads (~1.000€/Mo Budget)
5. **Google Analytics 4** — Tracking (0€)
6. **Canva Pro** — Designs & Factsheets (12€/Mo)
7. **Cal.com** — Terminbuchung (0€)
8. **Lemlist** — Cold Outreach (59€/Mo)

**Gesamt Software: ~150€/Monat** (+ Werbebudget)
